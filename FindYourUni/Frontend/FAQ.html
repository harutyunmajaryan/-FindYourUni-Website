Frequently Asked Questions

What information do I need to provide?

All we need is:

  • Your overall grade.
  • Your preferences from the options provided, prioritized based on your slider ratings.

Are my results guaranteed?

No. FindYourUni provides recommendations based on the information you provide. The results are only a guide to help narrow down your search. It is not a definitive ranking. We recommend conducting further research on each university.

How is my data used?

We do not share your personal information. Grades and preferences are used only to generate university recommendations.
